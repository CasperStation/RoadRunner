How To Buy

Create a Wallet

Download Sui Wallet or your wallet of choice from the app store or google play store for free. Desktop users, download the google chrome extension by going to Sui Wallet.io.

Get Some SUI

Have SUI in your wallet to switch to $BUNNY. If you don’t have any SUI, you can buy directly on Sui Wallet, transfer from another wallet, or buy on another exchange and send it to your wallet.

Go to MovePump

Connect to MovePump. Go to https://movepump.com/ in google chrome or on the browser inside your Sui Wallet app. Connect your wallet. Paste the $BUNNY token address into MovePump, select Road Runner, and confirm. When Sui Wallet prompts you for a wallet signature, sign.

Switch SUI for $BUNNY

Switch SUI for $BUNNY. We have very low taxes so you don’t need to worry about buying with a specific slippage, although you may need to use slippage during times of market volatility.
